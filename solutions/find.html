Find — telematics with sensor intelligence

Everything in FIND, plus configurable sensors and M2M integrations to turn field signals into actions.

The gap we close

Operations don't fail on averages — they fail on exceptions. Find captures the critical few signals (doors, temperature, drum rotation, PTO) and turns them into alarms and workflows.

Where it fits

  • Temperature/door/drum/PTO monitoring for regulated cargo and ready-mix
  • Asset tagging and worksite visibility for tools and small equipment
  • Custom dashboards for exception management and audits

What customers achieve

Teams respond in minutes, not days. Exceptions become measurable, reportable and improvable.

Utilities contractor — fewer missing assets, clearer ETAs

Problem

Small assets misplaced; arrivals unpredictable; customers in the dark.

Intervention

Tagged tools, geofenced job sites, automated depart/arrive events with alerting.

Outcome

30% fewer missing-asset tickets, more accurate ETAs and faster job close-outs.

The problem we solve

Late deliveries, opaque timelines and fuel waste don't just hurt margins — they break trust. FIND gives live context on routes, sites and behavior so teams can intervene early.

How FIND works (at a glance)

  • Live tracking & geofences on depots, customer sites and risk zones
  • Driver behavior: speeding, harsh events, idling, rest
  • Route & job status timelines for dispatch and CS
  • Utilization & downtime analytics (vehicle, driver, route)
  • Maintenance reminders & odometer rules
  • Connectors & APIs to your TMS/ERP/BI

What customers achieve

Higher on-time performance, lower fuel spend, fewer disputes. Dispatch can see what's late before customers do.

National logistics fleet — from firefighting to foresight

Problem

300-vehicle fleet with frequent late deliveries and rising fuel.

Intervention

Introduced FIND with customer-site geofences, driver behavior alerts and route adherence dashboards.

Outcome

12% improvement in on-time delivery and ~8% fuel reduction within 4 months; exception-to-resolution time dropped from 3 days to same-day.
