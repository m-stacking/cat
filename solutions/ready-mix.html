Ready-Mix — end-to-end concrete logistics

From batching to pour — orders, loads, drum status, travel time and site events — to cut waiting time and waste.

The concrete reality

A few minutes of idle at plant or site multiplies across the day. Disputes follow. Ready-Mix surfaces the full chain, so batching, logistics and site teams share one version of the truth.

Capabilities

  • Dispatch-to-delivery status in real time
  • Drum rotation & slump-time proxies (via sensor inputs)
  • Route optimization & site turnaround insights
  • Exceptions: delays, diversions, idling

What customers achieve

Fewer disputes, better asset turns and more predictable cycles. The data explains the day.

Regional supplier — turning time into capacity

Problem

Plant and site queueing caused long cycles and finger-pointing.

Intervention

End-to-end visibility; drum rotation monitoring; site turnaround analytics.

Outcome

Average cycle time reduced by 14%; fewer waiting-time disputes with time-stamped events.
