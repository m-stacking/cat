FIND Plus — telematics with sensor intelligence

Everything in FIND, plus configurable sensors and M2M integrations to turn field signals into actions.

The gap we close

Operations don't fail on averages — they fail on exceptions. FIND Plus captures the critical few signals (doors, temperature, drum rotation, PTO) and turns them into alarms and workflows.

Where it fits

  • Temperature/door/drum/PTO monitoring for regulated cargo and ready-mix
  • Asset tagging and worksite visibility for tools and small equipment
  • Custom dashboards for exception management and audits

What customers achieve

Teams respond in minutes, not days. Exceptions become measurable, reportable and improvable.

Utilities contractor — fewer missing assets, clearer ETAs

Problem

Small assets misplaced; arrivals unpredictable; customers in the dark.

Intervention

Tagged tools, geofenced job sites, automated depart/arrive events with alerting.

Outcome

30% fewer missing-asset tickets, more accurate ETAs and faster job close-outs.
