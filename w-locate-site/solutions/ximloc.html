XimLoc — SIM-based positioning without GPS

Accurate, scalable device location using cellular network signals. Works indoors, in dense urban areas and with minimal battery impact.

Where GPS falls short

Indoors, urban canyons and low-power devices are blind spots for GPS. XimLoc reads network signals to locate SIM-based devices and phones when GPS can't.

What XimLoc does

  • Position SIM-based devices/phones via cellular signals
  • Indoor and urban-canyon friendly; complements GPS
  • OS/device agnostic with minimal battery impact
  • Ideal for workforce safety, compliance and coarse asset tracking

What customers achieve

Reliable location indoors and at scale — from lone-worker safety to compliance reporting.

Mobile workforce safety — coverage where it matters

Problem

Technicians spent hours indoors where GPS failed. Compliance suffered.

Intervention

XimLoc positioning with indoor-friendly signals; safety check-ins; geofence alerts on risky sites.

Outcome

Consistent indoor location traces; improved compliance; fewer missed check-ins.
